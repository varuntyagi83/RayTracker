# Voltic — Meta Ad Intelligence Platform

**Voltic** is an all-in-one Meta (Facebook & Instagram) advertising intelligence platform that unifies campaign analytics, competitor research, automated reporting, and AI-powered creative generation in a single workspace.

Think of it as **Supermetrics + AdSpy + Jasper** — without the three separate subscriptions.

---

## What Voltic Does

Voltic helps performance marketers and growth teams:

- **Monitor campaign performance** across all Meta ad accounts in one dashboard
- **Discover competitor ads** from Meta's Ads Library and save them for inspiration
- **Automate Slack reports** so your team wakes up to insights, not dashboards
- **Generate AI ad variations** based on competitor creatives + your own products
- **Track competitor strategies** with AI-written intelligence reports
- **Analyse video ads** frame-by-frame with AI hook and narrative extraction

---

## Core Features

### Dashboard
A live overview of your advertising performance across all connected Meta ad accounts.

- Revenue, spend, and profit KPIs for today, yesterday, and the last 7 days
- Top-performing creatives, headlines, ad copy, and landing pages ranked by ROAS
- Automatically shows demo data until you connect a real Meta account

---

### Discover — Meta Ads Library Browser
Browse competitor ads from Meta's public Ads Library without leaving Voltic.

- Search any brand by name to see their active ads
- Filter by platform (Facebook, Instagram, Messenger) and format (image, video, carousel)
- View ad copy, creative assets, estimated impressions, and run dates
- Save any ad to a Board for your swipe file
- Generate AI insights on why an ad works
- Compare two ads side-by-side with an AI summary of key differences
- Save a brand as a competitor for ongoing monitoring

---

### Boards — Swipe File Collections
Organise saved competitor ads into named collections.

- Create boards for different themes, competitors, or campaigns
- Thumbnail previews of every saved ad at a glance
- Rename, reorganise, and delete boards as needed

---

### Automations — Scheduled Slack Reports
Set up automated reports delivered directly to your Slack channels. Three report types:

**Performance Digest**
Daily or weekly summary of your top and bottom-performing campaigns, creatives, headlines, and landing pages. Choose which metrics matter (ROAS, spend, revenue, CTR) and when to receive them.

**Competitor Intelligence**
Weekly scrape of a competitor's Meta ads, delivered to Slack with impressions, platforms, and AI commentary. Know when a competitor launches a new campaign without checking manually.

**Comment Monitoring**
Hourly digest of new comments on your Meta ads. Surface negative feedback, urgent questions, and trending sentiment before they escalate.

Each automation is configured through a step-by-step wizard: choose type → configure settings → set schedule → pick Slack channel → activate.

---

### Campaign Analysis
Deep-dive analytics for every campaign across all connected ad accounts.

- Table view of all campaigns with spend, ROAS, revenue, impressions, CTR, and purchases
- Date range filtering (7 days, 30 days, 90 days, or custom)
- Performance trend charts: revenue vs. spend, ROAS over time
- Drill down into individual campaign creatives and their metrics
- Compare two campaigns side-by-side
- Export any campaign report as a PDF

---

### Creative Studio — AI Ad Strategy Chat
An AI chat interface for creative brainstorming, grounded in your brand and product context.

- Start multiple conversation threads on different campaigns or topics
- Choose your AI provider: OpenAI GPT-4o, Google Gemini, or Anthropic Claude
- Conversations are saved and searchable
- Use it to ideate hooks, refine copy, develop creative briefs, or pressure-test campaign strategy

---

### Variations — AI Ad Creative Generation
Generate new ad concepts by combining a competitor's creative with your own product.

**How it works:**
1. Select a competitor ad from Discover (or paste a brief)
2. Choose a product from your Assets catalog
3. Pick a variation strategy:
   - **Hero Product** — product front and centre
   - **Curiosity** — open loop, creates intrigue
   - **Pain Point** — problem to solution narrative
   - **Proof Point** — social proof and credentials
   - **Image-Only** — visual-led, minimal text
   - **Text-Only** — copy-heavy format
4. Set creative direction: product angle, lighting, background style, aspect ratio
5. Add optional notes for the AI
6. Generate — AI produces a headline, body copy, and image composition
7. Save or download the result

Brand guidelines are applied automatically if configured. Each generation costs credits from your workspace balance.

### Assets — Product Catalog
Upload and manage the products used in AI variation generation.

- Upload a product image, name, and description
- Assign brand guidelines to individual products
- Generate AI-enhanced backgrounds for product shots
- Search and filter your catalog
- All assets are available when generating variations

---

### Brand Guidelines — AI Creative DNA
Define your brand identity so AI-generated content stays on-brand.

- Step-by-step wizard captures brand name, voice, colour palette, target audience, and design rules
- Attach logos and visual references
- Assign guidelines to specific products in your Assets catalog
- Set a default guideline that applies to all AI generation
- Variations and insights automatically respect your guidelines

---

### Competitors — AI Intelligence Reports
Track competitors and generate deep-dive strategy reports.

- Add any Facebook/Instagram page as a competitor
- Generate an AI report analysing their ad strategy: messaging themes, target audience inference, creative patterns, estimated performance, and recommended angles to outcompete them
- Reports are stored and searchable
- Credit cost: 3 base credits + 2 credits per ad analysed

---

### Video Analysis — Ad Decomposition
AI analysis of competitor video ads, frame by frame.

- Paste a competitor video ad link
- AI extracts: opening hook technique, scene-by-scene narrative, text overlays, scroll-stop score, pacing and music impact
- Understand what makes a video ad work before you try to beat it
- Credit cost: 4 credits per analysis

## Getting Started

### Step 1 — Sign Up
Go to the Voltic app and sign up with your email or Google account. You'll be prompted to name your workspace (e.g. "Acme Marketing" or "My Agency").

### Step 2 — Connect Meta
In **Settings**, connect your Meta business account via OAuth. Voltic will sync your ad accounts, campaigns, creatives, and metrics. This is required for the Dashboard, Campaign Analysis, and Reports to show real data.

### Step 3 — Connect Slack (optional)
In **Settings**, connect your Slack workspace. This is required for Automations to deliver reports to your channels.

### Step 4 — Add Your Products
In **Assets**, upload product images and descriptions. These are used when generating ad variations.

### Step 5 — Set Brand Guidelines (optional but recommended)
In **Brand Guidelines**, define your brand voice, colours, and design rules. AI-generated content will respect these settings.

### Step 6 — Explore
- **Browse Discover** to find competitor ads and save them to boards
- **Create an Automation** to receive a daily performance digest on Slack
- **Generate a Variation** to test a new creative angle
- **Run a Competitor Report** to understand what your rivals are doing

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Do I need a Meta ad account to use Voltic?**
No. The Discover, Boards, Competitors, Video Analysis, and Creative Studio features work without a connected Meta account. Dashboard, Campaign Analysis, and Reports require a connected account to show real data (they display demo data otherwise).

**How many Meta ad accounts can I connect?**
Voltic supports up to 91 connected Meta ad accounts per workspace.

**Is my data private?**
Yes. All data is isolated to your workspace. Voltic uses Supabase Row Level Security to ensure your data is never accessible to other workspaces.

**What happens when I run out of credits?**
AI features will be unavailable until you top up. All other features (dashboard, reports, campaign analysis, discover, boards, automations) continue to work without credits.

**Can multiple team members use the same workspace?**
Yes. Workspace members can be added to share access to the same data, boards, automations, and settings.

**What countries does the Meta Ads Library search support?**
All countries are supported. You can search for brand ads from any geography — the default is global (ALL).
